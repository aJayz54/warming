Hi! Welcome to the Climate Change Tracker! This is an interactive website I built to help monitor different metrics to help you better understand what climate change is and what the dangers of our current trajectory are. On this website, you'll find a lot of information about a few ways to track climate change: carbon emissions, ice extent, ocean pH, as well as simply global temperatures. But don't just look at the numbers: We're already feeling some of the impacts of climate change, and more is sure to come! Rising sea levels, the recent wildfire in California, and the devastation of certain marine ecosystems are just a few tangible impacts of climate change that you can visibly see right now, and those are impacts that aren't necessarily quantifiable with metrics like these. Below, you can see another very tangible impact of climate change: rising global temperatures. If you've noticed a recent surge in heat waves, or have generally just felt that everything is getting a little bit hotter, this is why! This website is just meant as a tool to help you track where the most recent data is, as well as a quick guide on what these metrics mean and what they actually affect in the real world.

Anyway, navigating to any of the tabs on the top left will let you start learning about climate change metrics! Enjoy!
